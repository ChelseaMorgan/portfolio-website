About Me

My Story

I'm Chelsea! I'm a UX/UI designer based out of NYC aiming to craft solutions to make the digital world a more inclusive and user-friendly place. My journey into UX design was a bit unconventional. Originally a medical Speech- Language Pathologist by trade, I discovered the field of UX design through my experience with a variety of electronic medical record and billing systems required for my work, where I witnessed the impact that good - and bad - design can have on real-world outcomes. Now after transitioning into the field of UX design, I'm using my unique background to merge human-centered principles, functionality, and aesthetics to create accessible and inclusive digital solutions that just work.
